\section*{Finite local type space}
Let $\mathcal{T}$ denote the set of FO$^k$-types of rooted radius-$2R$ neighborhoods in $\Delta$-bounded graphs.
Then $M:=|\mathcal{T}|<\infty$ depends only on $(\Delta,k,R)$.
For each $v\in V(G)$ let $\tau(v)\in\mathcal{T}$ be the FO$^k$-type of $(B_{2R}(v),v)$.
Partition $V(G)=\bigsqcup_{i=1}^M V_i$ where $V_i:={v:\tau(v)=\tau_i}$.
