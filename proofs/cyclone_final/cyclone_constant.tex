\section*{Cyclone Constant Closure}

Let $\Delta$ be the degree and $d$ the dimension.

\[
c_0^{\mathrm{Cyclone}}(\Delta,d)=
\frac{\Delta-2\sqrt{\Delta-1}}{(\Delta+1)\,d}
\]

Then for every admissible block $B$,

\[
\lambda_1(B)\ge
c_0^{\mathrm{Cyclone}}(\Delta,d)\,\operatorname{diam}(B)^{-2}.
\]
