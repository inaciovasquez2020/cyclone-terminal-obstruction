# Strong Convergence Upgrade

## Weak limit
\[
g_L \rightharpoonup g \quad \text{in } L^2.
\]

## Energy decay
\[
\|\nabla g_L\|^2 \to 0.
\]

## Compact embedding (discrete blocks)
\[
g_L \to g \quad \text{strongly in } L^2.
\]

## Consequence
\[
\|g\| = \lim_{L\to\infty} \|g_L\| = 1.
\]

## Combined with rigidity
\[
\nabla g = 0 \Rightarrow g \equiv \text{constant}.
\]

## Orthogonality
\[
\int g = 0 \Rightarrow g = 0.
\]

## Contradiction
\[
\|g\| = 1 \ \text{and} \ g=0.
\]
